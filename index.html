I am an Applied Scientist at the AWS Deep Learning group led by Dr. Alex Smola. I received Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign under the supervision of Prof. Carl A. Gunter and Prof. Jiawei Han.

My past internships were with Google Research, Microsoft Research, Yahoo Labs, UBS, and proprietary trading. I also obtained M.S. in Statistics, M.S. in Computer Science, and B.Eng in Computer Science (GPA Rank: 1/214).
