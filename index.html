Aston Zhang



Applied Scientist
Amazon Web Services Deep Learning

I am an Applied Scientist at AWS. My research interests are in deep learning. I earned my Ph.D. in Computer Science from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign under the supervision of Prof. Carl A. Gunter and Prof. Jiawei Han.

My past internships were with Google Research, Microsoft Research, Yahoo Labs, UBS, and proprietary trading. I also obtained a second M.S. in Statistics and a B.Eng in Computer Science with a Business Minor (top GPA for all the degrees).
